Eagle
The Majestic Bird of Prey
Description:

Eagles are awe-inspiring birds of prey known for their powerful flight, keen eyesight, and striking appearance. With a diverse range of species found worldwide, these majestic creatures are revered for their symbolism and top predator status in various ecosystems.

Key Features:
  • Wingspan: Varies by species, with some exceeding 7 feet (2.1 meters).
  • Vision: Exceptional eyesight, estimated to be 4-8 times stronger than humans.
  • Talons: Sharp and powerful, used for hunting and grasping prey.
  • Habitat: Diverse, ranging from mountains and forests to coastal areas.
Behavior:

Eagles are known for their soaring flight, often reaching high altitudes. They build large nests in elevated locations and form monogamous pairs, displaying strong family bonds.

Diet:

As apex predators, eagles have a varied diet that includes fish, mammals, and other birds. Their hunting prowess helps maintain ecological balance.
